Mini Course 9: Introduction to Supply and Demand (Economics)

Supply and demand is a fundamental economic model that explains how prices vary based on the balance between product availability and consumer desire.

Concept        Definition  
Demand        Quantity consumers are willing to buy at various prices.  
Supply        Quantity producers are willing to sell at various prices.  
Equilibrium   Price point where supply equals demand.  
Surplus       Excess supply over demand at a given price.  
Shortage      Excess demand over supply at a given price.  

The interaction between supply and demand determines market equilibrium, influencing production decisions and price strategies.
